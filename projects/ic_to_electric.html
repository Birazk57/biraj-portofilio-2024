IC Engine to Electric Conversion

Vehicle: Maruti 800 | Location: Dhulikhel, Nepal
Duration: 1 Year | Tools: SolidWorks, MATLAB, Electric Drivetrain Components

Overview

This project converted a 28-year-old internal combustion engine vehicle (Maruti 800) into a battery-electric vehicle, aiming to reduce emissions and offer a scalable retrofitting model.

Process & Methodology

The conversion process involved dismantling the ICE system, designing and fabricating a custom electric drivetrain, and integrating battery packs with a control system. CAD tools were utilized to design replacement components, while MATLAB simulations validated performance prior to fabrication.

Key Results

  • Improved energy efficiency and reduced emissions post-conversion.
  • Successful integration of the electric drivetrain within the existing chassis.
  • Field testing confirmed practical viability for urban and rural applications.

Conversion Process Snapshot

Overall Impact

The conversion validated the technical feasibility of retrofitting older vehicles into clean electric alternatives, with significant environmental and economic benefits.
