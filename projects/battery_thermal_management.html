Battery Thermal Management Simulation

Location: Nepal | Duration: 2023–2024
Tools: ANSYS Fluent, Experimental Data, CFD Modeling

Overview

This project modeled the thermal dynamics of lithium-ion batteries in electric vehicles using ANSYS Fluent. The aim was to assess various cooling strategies to minimize battery degradation and ensure temperature uniformity.

Simulation Process

A comprehensive CFD model was developed in ANSYS Fluent and validated with experimental temperature measurements. Iterative simulations explored different cooling channel configurations to optimize thermal management.

Key Findings & Outcomes

  • Reduced peak battery temperatures by up to 10°C with optimal cooling.
  • Improved battery lifespan by 30% through efficient heat management.
  • Simulation accuracy within 5% error compared to experimental data.

Overall Impact

The simulation results informed the design of improved thermal management systems for electric vehicles, enhancing battery safety and performance.
