Energy Consumption & Range Detection

Location: Nepal | Duration: Multi-phase Research
Tools: MATLAB, Python, Data Analytics & Visualization Tools

Overview

This project analyzes the factors influencing energy consumption and driving range in electric vehicles under real-world conditions. By aggregating data on driving behavior, road elevations, and vehicle dynamics, a predictive model was built to estimate energy usage and state-of-charge (SOC) behavior.

Development Process

The process began with extensive data collection using onboard sensors and GPS data logging over various routes. MATLAB and Python were used to clean, process, and analyze the data. Regression techniques and machine learning algorithms were employed to develop predictive models that correlate energy consumption with driving conditions.

Key Results & Outcomes

  • Prediction accuracy with less than 6% error in SOC estimation.
  • Identified significant correlation between road elevation changes and increased energy usage.
  • Provided actionable insights for optimizing energy management in electric vehicle conversions.
